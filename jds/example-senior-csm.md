# Senior Customer Success Manager — Acme SaaS (Example)

**Location:** Singapore (hybrid)
**Type:** Full-time

## About the role

We're looking for a Senior CSM to own a portfolio of 8–12 enterprise accounts across Southeast Asia. You'll be the primary post-sales relationship owner — responsible for onboarding, adoption, renewal, and expansion.

## What you'll do

- Own end-to-end customer lifecycle: onboarding through renewal and upsell
- Build executive relationships at Director and C-suite level
- Run quarterly business reviews and present ROI data to senior stakeholders
- Partner with Product to surface customer feedback and influence roadmap
- Identify expansion opportunities and collaborate with Sales on commercial proposals
- Manage a portfolio of S$2M+ ARR

## What we're looking for

- 5+ years in Customer Success, Account Management, or a client-facing role in B2B SaaS
- Demonstrated track record of hitting NRR targets (110%+ preferred)
- Experience managing enterprise accounts (100+ seat deployments)
- Strong executive presence — comfortable presenting to CIOs and Division Directors
- Familiarity with CS platforms (Gainsight, Totango, or similar)
- Experience with government or regulated-industry clients is a strong plus
- Singapore PR or Citizen preferred

## Compulsory application questions

1. Describe a time you turned around an at-risk account. What was the situation, what did you do, and what was the outcome?
2. How do you measure the health of an account? What leading indicators do you rely on?
3. Give an example of influencing a product roadmap based on customer feedback.
